A Playlist For Your Own Convenience

How This Works: On this website you can make your own playlist by submitting a link from YouTube. Fill out the form below to add a song, and make sure to include the songs basic details. To view the songs that were pre-added for you one by one, click the "Display Song" button below. However, if you are impatient, (like me), and would like to see all of the pre-added songs at once, click the "Display All Songs" button below.
